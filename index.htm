How This Guide Will Help You

Comprehensive Learning - No Prior Experience Needed

This guide will teach you how to install, set up and use UI.Vision in a simple and easily understood way. It will guide you through your first few macros, to help you along your path to mastering this unique way of coding.

 

Practical, Step-By-Step Examples

In this guide, you will walk through examples which require little to no additional set-up.

Soon, youll be able to apply your new knowledge into the real world, helping your understanding. I have also attached a lot of images to help you along the way, and to make sure that you havent missed a step.

 

Macros are Plain Useful!

The valuable skills you will learn from this guide will help you in your everyday life, helping your productivity. Not to mention your efficiency (aka. laziness) as you can really lower your workload in some lucky circumstances.

Also, your macros arent one-time tricks! You will always have them and the skills to make them, so you can break free from doing the same, simple things over and over every day.

 

Similar Functionalities to Other Coding Languages

UI.Vision uses its own coding language so it mirrors many common functionalities of many other languages. Labels from Low-Level languages like ARM and x86, echo from Linux/Unix alongside the basics like traditional loops from most modern programming languages.

 

If you are aware of the origin of these parallels, it will make UI.Vision coding a lot easier.

If you arent, this will give you a good understanding of these functionalities in their original languages, making you an overall better and more capable programmer.
